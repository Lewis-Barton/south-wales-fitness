How to Join?

If you are interested in joining us then there are a few ways in which you can go about this. You can sign up over the phone or by email. Alternatively, you can come into the centre and get signed up whilst you are there. For both of these options it is required that after you have been signed up that you complete a brief orientation process and the policies in place while in the facilities.

Is personal training available?

There are personal trainers that operate out of our facilities that can be booked for sessions within the centre if you so wish. The contact information and pricing for these individuals can be found at the front desk.

How can I change my membership?

If at any point you wanted to change the membership that you are currently on (you want to upgrade from basic to enhanced or vice versa). All you would have to do is contact the gym requesting this to be completed, we will then process this for you. Depending on when your previous membership was due to renew we would calculate if any money is owed to you, or required for the new membership, this will be disclosed during the process.

How soon do I have to book classes?

We recommend booking classes as soon as possible if you would like to guarantee your space for that time and date, however you can book classes as little as 1 hour prior to the start time. If you were not able to book the class beforehand, you can still come into the gym to see if there is space available for the class, this is not a guarantee that we can accomdate you.

How much do classes cost if I don't have the enhanced membership?

All classes will cost £5 if you do not have the enhanced membership option, with the exception to the yoga classes that are slightly less at £3 per session. These fee's will need to be paid on the day of the class, and the sessions cannot be attended if they have not been paid.

What do I do if I have left something at the gym?

If you think that you have left something in the gym then please contact us straight away. Our staff regularly check the gym and facilities available and if anything is found they will put it in safe keeping away from the rest of the public. If we do have the item that you have lost then you would need to arrange a suitable time for this to be collected from the front desk.

Disclaimer

It is a requirement for all members or non-members that are using the facilities to have previously taken the orientation session to ensure that all of the facilities are used correctly. Further to this, we are required to have attendees aged between 16 and 18 to attend an additional safety orientation for the correct use of equipment to be covered by our health and safety policy.
