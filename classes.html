Yoga

This class is perfect if you're looking to tone and strengthen your muscles in a nice relaxing way. Consisting of deliberate and concentrated movements this is a relaxing and quiet routine for calming while also building up muscle tone. We offer a variety of different yoga classes every week so make sure to see which one is for you.

Circuit Training

This class is perfect if you're looking for a fast-paced workout ideal for burning calories and strength-training. In this class you will be doing a variety of short interval exercises, swapping between each exercises every minute with a small break between each set. This can be done with any type of equipment that we have available at the gym, and they are always guaranteed to give you a great workout.

HIIT

Our HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) classes are great for anyone that is looking to burn a serious number of calories while working on your cardio movements at the same time. Little to no equipment is needed for this one, so if you're new and looking to throw yourself right into it then this is the one for you!

Spinning

Our spin classes are always going to get you pumped up! These sessions are great for getting in your cardio and burning some serious calories. The instructors that we have for these are perfect for anyone, offering great advice on what you can do to adjust the session to your level of experience and always keeping you energized to keep pushing through.
