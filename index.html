South Wales Fitness

Located within Barry, South Wales Fitness established its roots in 2009. Starting as a small family business, it quickly grew into the brilliant and well equipped leisure center that you can see today. With over 100 gym equipments, an olympic sized swimming pool, 2 sports halls that can be hired with equipment for multiple activities.

Enjoy a swim in our olympic sized pool!

This 50m pool is perfect for any needs, it has been split evenly for lane swimmers and casual swims. It is capable of holding large events for private hire, or private classes. Fully equipped with changing rooms, showers, and lockers so you can swim in comfort.

Hire out our sports halls!

We have two large sports halls that can be hired for further activities. Already fully equipped for football, basketball, or gymnastics these halls are perfect for clubs or sessions as a group. For all bookings please contact the center directly.
