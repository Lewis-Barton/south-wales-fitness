South Wales Fitness

Located within Barry, South Wales Fitness established its roots in 2009. Starting as a small family business, it quickly grew into the brilliant and well-equipped leisure centre that you can see today. With over 100 gym equipment’s, an Olympic sized swimming pool, 2 sports halls that can be hired with equipment for multiple activities.

Enjoy a swim in our olympic sized pool!

This 50m Olympic pool is perfect for any occasion, it can be split evenly to accommodate continuous lane swimmers and casual swims when it is needed. It is constantly under watch by our fully trained lifeguards, ensuring that everyone can have a safe and enjoyable swim. We also offer lockers, as well as our fully equipped changing rooms and shower facilities that are free for use to anyone that is using the pool.

If you are bringing some younger swimmers with you, we also have a separate children’s pool, which has depths between 1ft to 4ft that are clearly marked on the walls.

Capable of holding large events, you can hire the pool for a fee, and we are even able to supply hire of inflatables as well if this is what is wanted for your event. For more information on the prices, and availability of private hire, please contact the front desk.

Hire out our sports halls!

Within the centre we also have two exceptionally large sports halls that are suitable for a whole range of activities. Each hall can be separated into different sections if you would like to run more than one session at a time.

Already fully equipped for football, basketball, or gymnastics these halls are perfect for club/group sessions. Included within the hiring fee, we can also supply some additional equipment where required (balls for football or basketball).

For all booking information, availability, and prices please contact the front desk.
